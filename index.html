Help To Help
There are many ways you can help a needy child in need. One way is to donate food items and supplies to starving children. You can include money for safe drinking water, for example, if you only want to donate goods as compassion gifts that won't expire. There are many different ways this type of donation can be done since there might be a platform for you to set up a face or even event to teach your community about the world's poor children who may not have even food on their plate during meal time.
